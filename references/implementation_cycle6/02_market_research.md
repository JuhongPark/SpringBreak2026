# Market Research - Cycle 6 (2026-02-25)

## Focus
Operator-centered alert tuning and actionable diagnostics.

## Sources reviewed
- PagerDuty alert fatigue guidance: https://www.pagerduty.com/resources/learn/what-is-alert-fatigue/
- Google SRE monitoring and alerting workbook: https://sre.google/workbook/monitoring/

## Patterns extracted
1. Alert thresholds should be tunable for environment and scenario context.
2. Diagnostics should include next-step recommendations, not only rule violations.
3. Severity distribution helps prioritize response quickly.
