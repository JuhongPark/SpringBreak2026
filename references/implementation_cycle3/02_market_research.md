# Market Research - Cycle 3 (2026-02-25)

## Focus
Operational observability UX for faster issue triage.

## Sources reviewed
- Honeycomb observability concepts: https://www.honeycomb.io/blog/observability-3-pillars
- OpenTelemetry semantic conventions overview: https://opentelemetry.io/docs/concepts/signals/

## Patterns extracted
1. Keep raw events for depth, but expose an immediate aggregate view for speed.
2. Failure and duration are first-pass triage indicators.
3. Summary endpoints reduce cognitive load during operational checks.
