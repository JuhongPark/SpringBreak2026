# Market Research - Cycle 5 (2026-02-25)

## Focus
Alerting and anomaly patterns in observability workflows.

## Sources reviewed
- Prometheus alerting concepts: https://prometheus.io/docs/alerting/latest/overview/
- Grafana alerting fundamentals: https://grafana.com/docs/grafana/latest/alerting/

## Patterns extracted
1. Keep threshold-based anomaly detection explicit and configurable.
2. Severity labels help triage (warning vs critical).
3. Anomaly summaries should be separate from raw traces for operator speed.
